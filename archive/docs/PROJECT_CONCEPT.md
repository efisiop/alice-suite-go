## üéØ What Is Alice Suite?

**Alice Suite** is a **companion app** for students reading the physical book "Alice's Adventures in Wonderland."

### Simple Explanation

- Student reads from **physical book** (the real book)
- Student encounters a word they don't understand
- Student opens the companion app on their phone/tablet
- Student looks up the word and gets instant help
- Student continues reading the physical book

**Key Point:** The app helps with the physical book. It does NOT replace the physical book.

---

## üéØ Core Purpose

Make reading classic literature easier by providing instant help when students get stuck, without interrupting their reading flow.

---

## üë• Who Uses It?

### Primary User: Students
- Ages 12-18+ reading classic literature
- Need help understanding old words and phrases
- Want to read the physical book but need support

### Secondary User: Teachers/Consultants
- Help students when they're stuck
- See which students need help
- Track student progress

---

## üèóÔ∏è Three Levels of Help

### Level 1: Instant Dictionary
- Student looks up a word
- Gets definition instantly
- Continues reading

### Level 2: AI Help
- Student asks a question about what they're reading
- AI explains it in simple terms
- Student understands and continues

### Level 3: Human Help
- Student still confused after AI help
- Student asks for human consultant
- Real person helps them
